Tasky Privacy Policy

This Chrome extension does not collect any personal data, and all task logs are stored locally, on the user's machine.

Tasky is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data:

Tasky collects task data, including duration and timestamps, stored locally on your device using Chrome's local storage API.

Data is used only for Tasky's functionality, like showing task history and goal notifications.

Tasky does not share your data with third parties. It stays on your device unless you export it (e.g., copying task history).

Tasky requires permissions for notifications and local storage, used only for core features.

You can delete your task data anytime via the History page or by clearing Chrome's local storage.

This policy may be updated as Tasky evolves. Check this page for updates.
